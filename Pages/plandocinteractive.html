Internet users by country in millions (Statista , 2022)

The first thing I did to start the process of designing this chart was to do research on the topic I wanted the interactive chart to convey. I chose the topic of the number of internet users by country in millions in 2022, I chose this topic because the internet plays a big role of a catalyst in the sharing and receiving of information between human beings. We live in an era where information is no longer spread or conveyed through word of mouth but we use social media to do that on a daily basis through the internet, this is because it is easy to use and it spreads information to/from all parts of the world effortlessly.

I used Statista's Research Department's evaluations on countries with the highest number of internet users in 2022, because the information was not confusing and it was legible. The names of the countries are France, Italy, Thailand, and Bangladesh (Statista Research Department, 2022); from the investigations Italy has the lowest population of internet users compared to the other three countries, while France has the highest number of internet users than the other three countries. For the design of the chart put the names of the countries on the x axis and the number of users measured in millions on the y axis, and displayed the results freely without any chronological order because I wanted the user to interact with the graph easily and make the interaction fun and understandable.

Reference:

  • Statista Research Department. 2022. Countries with the highest number of internet users in 2022.. Statista. Online. URL: https://www.statista.com/statistics/262966/number-of-internet-users-in-selected-countries/. (Accessed 08 October 2022). Statista
